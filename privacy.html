Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Last Updated: April 15, 2026

Contents

1. Data We Collect 2. How We Use Your Data 3. Cookies & Tracking 4. Data Sharing & Third Parties 5. Security Measures 6. Your Rights 7. Children's Privacy 8. Policy Updates 9. Contact Us

1. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, as well as data generated through your use of our services:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, password (encrypted), shipping/billing addresses
  • Transaction Data: Order history, payment method details (processed securely via Stripe/Razorpay), delivery preferences
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, products viewed, search queries, device information, IP address, browser type
  • Communication Data: Customer support messages, newsletter preferences, feedback submissions

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information helps us provide, improve, and personalize your shopping experience:

  • Process and fulfill orders, including shipping and delivery updates
  • Manage your account, wishlist, and saved preferences
  • Send order confirmations, shipping notifications, and service announcements
  • Personalize product recommendations and marketing communications (opt-in)
  • Analyze site usage to improve functionality, security, and user experience
  • Prevent fraud, enforce terms, and comply with legal obligations

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance functionality and analytics:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for cart functionality, session management, and security
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (aggregated, anonymized)
  • Personalization Cookies: Remember your preferences, size selections, and recently viewed items
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads and measure campaign effectiveness (only with consent)

You can manage cookie preferences anytime through your browser settings or our interactive Privacy Preference Center below.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We never sell your personal data. We may share limited information with trusted partners solely to operate our business:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe, Razorpay (for secure transaction handling)
  • Shipping Partners: DHL, FedEx, India Post (for order fulfillment)
  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics, Plausible (anonymized traffic data)
  • Legal/Compliance: When required by law, regulation, or to protect our rights

All third-party partners are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

5. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmissions
  • Secure, salted hashing for passwords (PBKDF2, 100,000+ iterations)
  • Regular security audits, vulnerability scanning, and penetration testing
  • Strict access controls and employee confidentiality agreements
  • Automated threat detection and incident response protocols

While no system is 100% immune to breaches, we continuously invest in protecting your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Withdraw consent for marketing communications or non-essential cookies
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data under certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@bevenu.com. We respond to all verified requests within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Bevenu is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately so we can delete the information.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on our website. Continued use of our services after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please reach out:

  • Email: privacy@bevenu.com
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@bevenu.com
  • Address: Bevenu Fashion Labs, New Delhi, India

We value your trust and are committed to transparent, responsible data practices.
