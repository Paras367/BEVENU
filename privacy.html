1. Data We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide you with a seamless, personalised shopping experience.

  • Account Data: Name, email address, encrypted password, shipping and billing addresses.
  • Transaction Data: Order history, payment method type (we never store full card numbers), delivery preferences, coupon usage.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, products viewed, search queries, cart contents, device type, browser, IP address (anonymised after 24h).
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through our contact form, support tickets, chat transcripts, newsletter preferences.

We never sell your personal data to third parties or advertisers — ever. Your information stays with Bevenu.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies. You control which are active via our Privacy Preference Centre below.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for cart, checkout, session security and fraud prevention. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Aggregated, anonymised traffic data to understand popular pages and search queries.
  • Personalisation Cookies: Remember your size preferences, recently viewed items, and layout choices.
  • Marketing Cookies: Deliver relevant ads across platforms. Only activated with explicit consent.

Manage all cookie preferences instantly using the interactive control panel at the bottom of this page.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We share the minimum necessary data with carefully vetted partners bound by strict data processing agreements:

  • Payment Processors: Razorpay, Stripe — secure transaction handling with PCI-DSS Level 1 compliance.
  • Shipping Partners: DHL, FedEx, India Post — order fulfilment and tracking only.
  • Analytics: Plausible Analytics (privacy-first, no personal identifiers shared).
  • Email Services: Transactional order emails only — never used for third-party marketing.
  • Legal & Compliance: When required by applicable law, court order, or to protect our rights.

We never share data with data brokers, advertising networks, or any party for resale purposes.

5. Security Measures

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption (HTTPS/TLS 1.3) for all data in transit.
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest in our databases.
  • Passwords hashed using bcrypt with 12+ salt rounds — never stored in plain text.
  • Regular penetration testing and third-party security audits (quarterly).
  • Strict access controls — only authorised personnel with legitimate business need can access your data.
  • Automated threat detection, rate limiting, and DDoS protection.
  • 72-hour breach notification in compliance with applicable data protection regulations.

7. Children's Privacy

Bevenu's services are not directed at individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@bevenu.com so we can delete the data promptly.

All kids' clothing products are curated with safety standards in mind. Our data practices extend this care to their parents and guardians.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • Prominent notice on our website homepage for 30 days following any change.
  • Email notification to your registered address for material changes.
  • In-app notification if you have a Bevenu account.

Continued use of our services after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. The version history is maintained internally — contact our DPO for previous versions.
