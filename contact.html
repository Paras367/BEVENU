Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. Log into your account and check "My Orders" for real-time status updates. For help, contact us with your Order ID.

What is your return policy?

We offer 30-day easy returns on all unworn, unwashed items with original tags. Initiate a return from your account or contact support. Return shipping is free for defective items.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to 50+ countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. All international orders include full customs documentation and tracking.

How can I collaborate with Bevenu?

We love working with creators and brands! Email dhimanparas605@gmail.com with your proposal, audience details, and ideas. Our team reviews all inquiries within 3–5 business days.

How do I find the right size?

Each product page has a detailed size guide with measurements in cm and inches. When in doubt, size up for relaxed fits. Our support team can help you choose — just drop us a message.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions. We never store card details on our servers. All payments are processed through Razorpay, India's most trusted payment gateway.
