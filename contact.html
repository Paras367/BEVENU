Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also log into your account and view order status under "My Orders". For assistance, contact us with your order ID.
What is your return policy?
We offer 30-day easy returns on all unworn, unwashed items with original tags. Initiate a return from your account or contact support. Return shipping is free for defective items.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to 50+ countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. All international orders include customs documentation and tracking.
How can I collaborate with Bevenu?
We love working with creators, brands, and influencers! Email partnerships@bevenu.com with your proposal, audience details, and collaboration ideas. Our team reviews all inquiries.
Is my data secure with Bevenu?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption, never sell your data, and comply with global privacy regulations. Read our full Privacy Policy for details on how we protect your information.
